Delete Your Data - Wrist Word

Last updated: June 15, 2026

If you want to delete your account and associated data from Wrist Word, you can request deletion by contacting us.

How to request data deletion

Please send an email to: support@mukhammed.labs.com

In your email, include the Google account email you used to sign in to the app.

What data will be deleted

  • Your account information (email address)
  • User profile data stored in Firebase
  • App usage-related data linked to your account

What data may be retained

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes for a limited period, if required by law.

Processing time

Data deletion requests are usually processed within 7–30 days.
